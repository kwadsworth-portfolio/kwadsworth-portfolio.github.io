About Me

Experienced Project Manager transitioning into cybersecurity, applying established delivery, risk, governance and compliance capability within a security-focused context.

I am an experienced Project Manager with a background in delivering complex, high-value infrastructure and technology projects. Over my career, I have worked across planning, risk management, governance, stakeholder engagement and delivery assurance within highly regulated and operationally critical environments.

Through this experience, I developed a strong interest in cybersecurity — particularly how security controls, risk management and governance frameworks underpin safe, resilient and compliant systems. This interest has led me to focus my career on cybersecurity, cloud security and GRC.

Rather than approaching cybersecurity purely from a theoretical perspective, I am building practical capability through structured labs and projects that cover:

  • Cloud security fundamentals across AWS and Azure.
  • Security logging, monitoring and basic detection using SIEM platforms.
  • Cyber risk management, governance and ISO 27001-style controls.
  • Security-focused automation using Python.

My project management background provides strong transferable skills that are directly relevant to cybersecurity and GRC roles, including structured problem-solving, risk-based decision making, documentation, stakeholder communication and the ability to work methodically within defined frameworks.

This portfolio documents my transition into cybersecurity and demonstrates both my existing professional experience and my commitment to continuous learning. My objective is to work in roles such as GRC analyst/manager, cyber project/programme manager or information security manager, where I can combine delivery discipline with security-focused thinking.
