About Me

My project management background provides strong transferable skills directly relevant to cybersecurity and GRC roles, including analytical problem-solving, risk-based decision making, documentation, communication and structured delivery.

This portfolio demonstrates my transition into cybersecurity and my commitment to developing strong technical foundations while applying established professional experience. I am targeting roles such as GRC analyst or manager, cyber project or programme manager, and information security management positions.
