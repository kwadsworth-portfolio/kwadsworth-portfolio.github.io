Project 3: Cyber GRC & Risk Management (Mini-ISMS)

Governance, risk and compliance initiative aligned to ISO 27001 principles, demonstrating how structured risk management supports effective security decision-making.

Objective

To design and implement a lightweight Information Security Management System (ISMS) for a small organisation, demonstrating how governance and risk management can be applied pragmatically without enterprise-level complexity.

What I Implemented

  • Asset identification and data classification aligned to business impact.
  • Risk register capturing threats, likelihood, impact and ownership.
  • Selection of proportionate security controls mapped to identified risks.
  • Drafted core information security policies and procedures.
  • Defined governance roles and review cadence.

Approach & Outcomes

  • Ensured traceability between risks, controls and governance objectives.
  • Applied structured project discipline to documentation and reviews.
  • Improved visibility of security risks for stakeholders.
  • Created a scalable ISMS foundation suitable for a growing organisation.

Tools & Artefacts

  • Risk register and control mapping.
  • ISMS policy set (access control, incident management, data protection).
  • Governance and review checklist.
